Our Story

Built on One Simple Idea

NI Drip Central Electronics & Appliances was founded in 2023 with one goal in mind — to make it easy for people across Belfast to get quality appliances without the high retail markups or the frustrating long delivery times.

We supply brand-new and professionally refurbished appliances from the UK's most trusted brands, including Samsung, LG, Bosch, Hotpoint, Beko and more. Every single item is tested and inspected by our team before it leaves the warehouse, so you can buy with total confidence.

Whether you're furnishing a family home, setting up a rental property, or simply replacing a broken appliance, we've got you covered with the right product at the right price — delivered straight to your door, often the very same day.

"We built NI Drip Central because people deserve access to quality appliances at fair prices, with service they can actually rely on. That's what we deliver, every single day."
