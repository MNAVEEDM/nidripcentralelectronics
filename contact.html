FAQs

Common Questions

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! Order before midday and we can often deliver the same day across Belfast and surrounding areas. Call us to confirm availability.

What does "refurbished" mean?

Our refurbished appliances are professionally cleaned, repaired where necessary, and fully tested so they work like new — at a fraction of the price.

Do appliances come with a warranty?

Yes, all our appliances come with a warranty. Please contact us for specific warranty details on individual products.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver across Belfast and Northern Ireland — including Bangor, Lisburn, Carrickfergus, Newtownabbey and more. Not sure? Just ask!

Do you offer installation?

Yes — ask us about our installation service when you order and we'll arrange for your appliance to be installed on delivery.

Can landlords order in bulk?

Absolutely. We work with many landlords and property managers across Belfast. We offer competitive bulk pricing — just give us a call to discuss.
